YOUR MISSION

Your planet is under attack from an endless stream of asteroids hurtling in from the right side of the screen. Your job is simple: shoot them down before they reach your world. If they hit the planet, you lose health. If health reaches zero, the planet is destroyed.

CONTROLS

🖱️ MOVE Aim your laser at asteroids
🖱️ CLICK Fire laser at cursor position
1 – 5 Activate powerups from your bar
S Open the shop (between waves)
P / ESC Pause the game
HOLD CLICK Continuous rapid fire
☄️

ASTEROIDS

Three types of asteroids threaten your planet. Bigger ones have more HP and deal more damage but also drop more coins!

🪨 SMALL 30–55 HP · 3–12 🪙 · Low damage
🪨 MEDIUM 80–130 HP · 8–18 🪙 · Medium damage
🪨 LARGE 180–260 HP · 15–28 🪙 · High damage
☄️ BOSS 600+ HP · 80–150 🪙 · Massive damage

💡 Tip: Prioritise large asteroids — they deal the most damage to your planet!

🪙

PICKUPS

Destroying asteroids drops coins and occasionally health packs that float across the screen. Move your cursor near them to attract them (or use the Coin Magnet powerup!).

🪙
COINS
Currency for the shop. Drops from every destroyed asteroid.
💚
HEALTH PACK
Restores 8–18 HP to your planet. Rare — 12% drop chance.

💡 Tip: Coins auto-collect if they drift close enough to your planet!

⚡

POWERUPS

Buy powerups from the shop and activate them with keys 1–5 during battle. They appear in your powerup bar at the bottom of the screen.

⚡ Rapid Fire – Triple fire rate for 15s
🔱 Triple Shot – 3 lasers at once for 12s
🛡️ Shield – Block the next asteroid hit
🧲 Coin Magnet – Attract all coins for 15s
💥 Nova Bomb – Instantly destroy all asteroids
❄️ Time Freeze – Freeze all asteroids for 5s
🤖 Auto Turret – AI turret shoots for you 20s
🌊

WAVES

Asteroids come in waves. As waves progress, asteroids spawn faster, move faster, have more HP, and come in greater numbers. Every 10 waves a BOSS ASTEROID appears. Between waves you have a brief window to visit the shop.
